Privacy Policy for SayItBetter

Effective Date: 8/11/2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for SayItBetter, an interactive language learning app. We value the trust you place in us and are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Voice Data: To deliver our Interactive Voice Response and Auditory Playback features, we collect and store voice recordings when users answer questions verbally. However, such data is deleted automatically within one minute.

3. How We Use Your Information

All collected data is solely used to offer language learning experiences for the user. The AI-generated responses are based on this voice data, but they are not stored.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your response data to any third parties. All data is strictly used to support the language learning experience within the SayItBetter app.

5. Security

We take your privacy seriously and have implemented stringent security measures to protect your data. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and if significant, we'll communicate these changes to our users.
